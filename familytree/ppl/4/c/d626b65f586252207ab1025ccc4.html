Narrative

From Hardwick Family Tree

"Robert John had a good upbringing, having private tutors and then onto public school which was the Grammar School Rotherham. He attend church regularly. Robert as a man was 6ft tall and had ginger hair. Robert never had a job, married Harriet Elizabeth Warlowe late in his life, (Harriet was known as lillian to her family and friends) he was Harriet's second husband. Harriet was a wealthy woman in her own right, Harriets first husband was a top boss at the British Steel works Rotherham, the family of Harriet Elizabeth Warlowe had the design patent of the Worlowe Furnaces used in the steel works.
